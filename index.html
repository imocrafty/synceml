Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SyncEML handles your data when you use our desktop application and its integration with Google APIs.

1. Use of Gmail API Scopes

Our application requests access to the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly scope. This is used exclusively to provide the following features:

  • Message Listing: To display your inbox or labels within the app.
  • Header Retrieval: To show Subject, Date, and Sender information.
  • Raw Content Download: To allow you to store email content locally on your device for personal archiving.

2. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SyncEML's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data Storage and Security

We believe in "Privacy by Design." Your data is handled as follows:

  • Local Only: All retrieved email data is stored locally on your own machine. We do not transmit your email content to our servers.
  • No Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties or for advertising purposes.

4. Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by deleting the application's local database or cache folder on your device.

5. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: imocrafty@gmail.com
